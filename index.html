About Apple FunToken

The Retro Neko (Cat) $RNK is a utility token intended for traditional DeFi staking protocols but also (and above all) to tackle INU (Dog) tokens.

Each week in addition to distributing APYs to our holders, a DAO will choose an INU token to attack by trading against its price and rewarding the sellers of the Dog Token targeted by airdrops.

Each wallet will have a limited number of stakes and the first 5,000 stakes will receive a unique NFT offering them the possibility of sharing part of the gains made by shorting the various dog tokens.
